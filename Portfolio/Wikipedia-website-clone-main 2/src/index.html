Andria Amiranashvili, (born March 12,2008) Georgia, specifically from Kutaisi He was born into a family of doctors. His mother, Maia Machitadze, is an anesthesiologist and reanimatologist from Kutaisi. His father, Givi Amiranashvili, is a surgeon and endoscopist, renowned in the city. Andria has two small sisters, Anina Amiranashvili and Aleqsandra Amiranashvili.

Education

Andria attended Niko Nikoladze School and continued his studies at the Physics and Mathematics School from the 5th grade onwards. Renowned for his academic prowess, he achieved a perfect score of 1600 in the SAT exam at the age of 16. With Andria's exceptional academic achievements and remarkable SAT score, he stands as a strong contender for admission to MIT.

Career

In 2024, Andria founded a website development company, initially comprising only two individuals. Since its inception, the company has grown significantly, now boasting a team of 20 skilled professionals.

Andria also holds the title of founding member of a rock band. With concerts and tours across the country, their music resonates through clips and videos. Andria's proficiency in electric guitar adds a professional touch to their performances.

Sport Career

Andria embarked on his basketball journey at a tender age, swiftly ascending to the top echelons of his city's age group. He competed in major championships, showcasing his prowess on the court.

Concurrently, he honed his strategic skills in chess, emerging as a five-time city champion and securing podium finishes in regional and national tournaments. In 2016, he even participated in the world chess championship, further cementing his dedication to both sports

Additionally, Andria remains an active participant in water polo, representing his city team with dedication and skill.
